# 🗺️ JP Reference Overview: White Mage (WHM) Macro UI Layouts

This document contains the translated macro row mappings for White Mage (WHM) 
from the [FF11マクロ研究所](https://hamham-fenrir.github.io/macroweb/) resource.

### ⚠️ Disclaimer on Completeness & Customization
While the reference author's grouping method is highly organized, it should be adapted 
to fit your personal muscle memory and controller/keyboard bindings.

---

## 📑 Multi-Page White Mage Macro Palette Layout

The original setup divides White Mage's spells across focused situational lines. 
It groups healing, status removal (-na spells), elemental buffs (Bar-spells), 
and enfeeblements.

### 👥 Common Key Terms & Abbreviations
* **単 (Cure Tiers):** Single-target Cure spells (e.g., 単３ = Cure III, 単６ = Cure VI).
* **ガ (Curaga Tiers):** AoE Curaga spells (e.g., ガ２ = Curaga II, ガ５ = Curaga V).
* **範プロ / 範シェ:** Protectra V / Shellra V (AoE variants).
* **単プロ / 単シェ:** Protect V / Shell V (Single-target variants).
* **光陣me:** Aurorastorm on self (`/ma "Aurorastorm" <me>`).
* **愛撫:** Sacrosanctity or Afflatus Solace/Misery interactive job abilities.
